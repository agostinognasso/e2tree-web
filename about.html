§ 2 · Publications

Peer-reviewed foundations.

The e2tree methodology is described in two journal articles covering the classification and regression settings respectively.

Computational Statistics · 2024 Read paper ↗

Explainable ensemble trees

Aria, M., Gnasso, A., Iorio, C., & Pandolfo, G.

Introduces the E2Tree algorithm for classification. Defines the co-occurrence dissimilarity framework, the recursive tree-growing procedure, and demonstrates the method on multiple real-world benchmarks. Shows that the single e2tree achieves classification accuracy comparable to the full Random Forest while remaining interpretable.

ASMBI · 2026 Read paper ↗

Extending Explainable Ensemble Trees to Regression Contexts

Aria, M., Gnasso, A., Iorio, C., & Fokkema, M.

Extends E2Tree to continuous response variables. Adapts the co-occurrence weighting to account for response similarity between observation pairs, and introduces a regression-specific impurity measure. Demonstrates the method on several regression benchmarks and provides a comparison against CART and GUIDE.
